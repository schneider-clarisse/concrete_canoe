About

The Team

The University of Waterloo will be competing annually at the Canadian National Concrete Canoe Competition (CNCCC), beginning with the 2014 competition in May. The team has stemmed off of the Waterloo Concrete Toboggan Team as a distinct entity - that is the canoe team is under a separate design, construction and fundraising process. It is led by the same captains whom have helped bring the toboggan team continued success at the Great Northern Concrete Toboggan Race (GNCTR) every year. Our drive and dedication led to five podium finishes at GNCTR 2013, and that same enthusiasm is being brought forward for CNCCC 2014 as we strive to achieve both results and to create a legacy at Waterloo of an annual concrete canoe team.

Succeeding at the competition requires more than just engineering, it requires the combined effort of Waterloo Engineering students from multiple disciplines including civil, mechanical and chemical engineering. Join the Team

The Concrete Canoe Team is always looking for interested, committed, and hard-working students to join the team! The Concrete Canoe Team is open to students from all engineering programs. We welcome everyone with any level of experience - we all started out with no idea with what we were doing!

As a member of the University of Waterloo Concrete Canoe Team, each student will develop a set of skills based on the level of commitment he or she has to the team. As a member, you will develop technical design skills in a variety of areas including concrete mix, concrete reinforcement, and canoe design. Members can be involved in all aspects of the team, including design, construction, project management, fundraising, recruiting sponsors, team promotion, and coordination of team spirit. Finally, the competition allows students to travel and represent Waterloo on a national level!
